The value of \( x^2 + 1 \) is always positive.

Sample Mathematics

We evaluate the integral

\[ \int_0^1 x^2 \, dx \]

Using basic calculus, we get

\[ \int_0^1 x^2 \, dx = \left[ \frac{x^3}{3} \right]_0^1 = \frac{1}{3} \]
